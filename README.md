# 📊 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer retention is one of the most critical challenges for subscription-based businesses, as acquiring a new customer is often more expensive than retaining an existing one. This project leverages Power BI to analyze customer demographics, service subscriptions, contract types, billing patterns, and tenure to uncover the key drivers of customer churn and recommend data-driven retention strategies.

The dashboard enables business stakeholders to interactively explore customer behavior, identify high-risk segments, and support strategic decision-making aimed at improving customer loyalty.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze customer churn patterns across different customer segments.
- Identify the key factors contributing to customer attrition.
- Evaluate the relationship between contracts, internet services, tenure, and churn.
- Provide actionable recommendations to improve customer retention.
- Support business decisions through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

- **Domain:** Telecommunications
- **Dataset Size:** 1,000 Customer Records
- **Source:** Kaggle
- **Data Preparation:** Cleaned and transformed using Power Query before visualization.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The interactive dashboard includes:

- Churn Analysis by Contract Type
- Churn Analysis by Internet Service
- Monthly Charges Comparison by Churn Status
- Customer Distribution by Gender
- Customer Tenure Analysis
- Decomposition Tree for Root Cause Analysis
- Interactive Slicers for Contract Type, Gender, and Internet Service

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

- Customers with **Month-to-Month contracts** showed the highest churn compared to long-term contracts.
- Customers using **Fiber Optic Internet** experienced a comparatively higher churn rate.
- Customers with **shorter tenure** were more likely to discontinue the service.
- Monthly charges showed a noticeable relationship with customer churn.
- The Decomposition Tree highlighted how multiple business factors collectively influence customer attrition.

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

- Encourage customers to migrate from Month-to-Month plans to long-term contracts through promotional offers.
- Improve service quality and customer support for Fiber Optic users.
- Develop targeted onboarding and engagement programs for newly acquired customers.
- Introduce personalized retention campaigns for customers identified as high churn risks.
- Continuously monitor churn metrics using interactive dashboards to support proactive decision-making.
